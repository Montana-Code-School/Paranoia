Are You Ready to Get Paranoid?

Greetings Agent,

You have been selected from our database to join a game of Paranoia. I am your moderator. In case this is your first time playing, let me explain how the game works.

How to play: Each agent will be assigned one target to eliminate, and each agent will in turn be pursued by another player. As no one knows who else may be playing, secrecy and stealth are paramount. (To maintain a low profile, be discreet when eliminating your target, and think twice before casually discussing the game with others.) All games begin and end at midnight.

Eliminations: When you eliminate an agent they will surrender their badge number to you. Enter this number on your profile page to confirm the deed and receive your new target. Follow the same proceedure if you are eliminated. (Note: Your badge number is assigned when you sign up. It can be found on your profile page.)

Rules: No weapons of any kind are allowed. Players are eliminated by touching with fingers. To maintain your cover, all eliminations must be covert. You may not enter a dorm room uninvited. All areas of campus are fair game except the following: classrooms, the library, and a player’s place of employment.

Keep in mind this is a game that rewards sneakiness and vigilance. Keep your identity concealed. The last agent standing is the winner, and gets full bragging rights within the Paranoia community. Play fair; don’t cheat. Above all, HAVE FUN!!'
